# GID - GEOGRAPHY

**World Overview:**
GID takes place on an Earth-like planet with modern technology (cars, internet, phones). The story unfolds in a fictional country/region where supernatural abilities (Nouryoku) exist alongside ordinary civilization. The main events occur across a small geographic area including an isolated island, coastal towns, and hidden faction headquarters.

## THE ISLAND

### **OVERVIEW**
An unnamed tropical island in isolation from the modern world. For the past 15 years, it has been home to Gallan Byronstrike and his two adopted children, Arjan and Lopney. Unknown to Gallan, the island also houses Free's hidden headquarters within its cliff system.

**Primary Setting For:**
- Arc 1: "A Wondrous Island"
- Arc 2: "The Land of the Free" (Free HQ discovery)

---

### **GEOGRAPHY**

#### **Size & Scale**
- Medium-to-large island
- Takes **multiple days to cross on foot**
- Large enough for diverse terrain and self-sufficient living
- Remote enough to remain undiscovered by modern civilization

#### **Climate**
- **Tropical** - warm and humid year-round
- Frequent rainfall maintains lush vegetation
- Occasional tropical storms (seasonal)
- Moderate temperature fluctuations between day and night

#### **Terrain & Biomes**
- **Dense tropical forest** covering most of the interior
- **River systems** providing fresh water flowing from interior to coast
- **Dramatic cliffs** on sea-facing sides (sheer drops to ocean)
- **Beaches** where forest meets ocean on calmer shores
- **Rolling elevation** with gentle hills throughout

#### **Flora & Fauna**
- Abundant fruit trees and edible plants throughout
- Wildlife suitable for hunting: birds, small game
- Rich fishing in rivers and coastal waters
- **No dangerous predators** - relatively safe environment for habitation
- Vibrant tropical ecosystem supporting self-sufficient living

### **KEY LOCATIONS**

#### **Gallan's Home**

**Location:**
- Built deep in the forest interior
- Close enough to the coast for ocean access (walking distance to shore)
- Sheltered by dense tree canopy
- Near a freshwater stream/river

**Structure:**
- Handbuilt wooden cabin constructed by Gallan 15 years ago
- Single-story structure with multiple rooms:
  - Gallan's private room
  - Arjan's room
  - Lopney's room
  - Common living/eating area
- Simple but functional design
- Sturdy construction to withstand tropical storms

**Surrounding Area:**
- **Training grounds:** Forest clearings within walking distance
  - Open spaces for ability practice and demonstrations
  - Natural obstacles (trees, rocks, uneven terrain) for combat/agility training
  - Multiple clearing sites for different training purposes
- **Garden:** Small cultivated area near the cabin for vegetables
- **Storage:** Simple structures for tools and supplies

**Resources & Utilities:**
- **Fully self-sufficient** - no connection to outside infrastructure
- **Water:** Fresh water from nearby river/stream
- **Food sources:**
  - Fishing (ocean and rivers)
  - Hunting (forest game)
  - Foraging (wild fruit and edible plants)
  - Small vegetable garden
- **No electricity** - lives completely off-grid
- **Lighting:** Candles, oil lamps, natural daylight
- **Cooking:** Wood fire/simple stove
- **Tools:** Basic hand tools, some brought by Gallan 15 years ago, others crafted from island materials

**The Boat:**
- Small boat built by Gallan from island timber
- Tied up on the shore nearest to the home
- Used for:
  - Fishing in deeper waters
  - Occasional supply runs to mainland
- **Supply runs destination:** Unnamed mainland settlement (NOT Kamino or Vista Alegre - those are too far)
- Gallan uses his Mental Erasure ability when visiting mainland - no one remembers seeing him
- Arjan and Lopney know the boat exists but are **not allowed to use it** (Gallan says it's too dangerous)

### **DAILY LIFE & SURVIVAL**

#### **Food Sources**
1. **Fishing**
   - Rivers: Freshwater fish
   - Ocean: Coastal and deep-water fishing (using Gallan's boat)
   - Year-round availability

2. **Hunting**
   - Small game animals in the forest
   - Birds
   - Trapping and bow hunting

3. **Foraging**
   - Wild fruit trees throughout the island
   - Edible plants and roots
   - Seasonal variations but generally abundant

4. **Gardening**
   - Small cultivated vegetable garden near Gallan's home
   - Supplements wild food sources
   - Tended by all three family members

#### **Water**
- Fresh river water (clean and drinkable)
- Multiple streams throughout the island
- No water scarcity
- Natural filtration through forest ecosystem

#### **Typical Daily Routine (Arjan & Lopney)**
- **Morning:** Training with Gallan
  - Ability control practice (for Arjan)
  - Martial arts (especially for Lopney)
  - Combat drills and sparring
- **Afternoon:** Survival activities
  - Hunting, fishing, gathering
  - Maintenance work on cabin and tools
  - Exploring the island
- **Evening:** Family time
  - Preparing and eating meals together
  - Rest and conversation
  - Simple entertainment (storytelling, stargazing)
- **Free time:**
  - Arjan and Lopney sparring/playing together
  - Individual exploration
  - Relaxation

#### **Training Areas**
- **Primary location:** Forest clearings near Gallan's home
- **Features:**
  - Open spaces for ability practice (Arjan's energy spheres)
  - Room for full-speed combat drills
  - Natural obstacles: trees, rocks, uneven ground
  - Used for agility training and tactical movement
- **Multiple sites:** Different clearings for different purposes
- **Accessibility:** All within reasonable walking distance of home

### **ISOLATION & ACCESS**

#### **Geographic Isolation**
- **Very far** from Kamino (multiple days by boat)
- **Even farther** from Vista Alegre (additional distance beyond Kamino)
- **Completely off modern maps** and shipping routes
- No accidental discovery by passing ships or aircraft

#### **Why It Remains Hidden**
1. **Remote location** - far from established sea lanes
2. **Gallan's Mental Erasure ability** - extends to the island itself
   - People who might notice it simply don't register its existence
   - Ships/planes that pass nearby fail to perceive it
3. **No strategic value** - appears as unremarkable empty ocean on maps
4. **No search efforts** - no one is looking for it

#### **Gallan's Mainland Trips**
- **Destination:** Unnamed small settlement/trading post on distant mainland
  - NOT Kamino or Vista Alegre (those are too far)
  - Closer access point for basic supplies
- **Frequency:** Occasional (every few months, as needed)
- **Method:** Small boat, traveling alone
- **Purpose:** Acquiring items that can't be made/found on island
  - Medicine
  - Metal tools
  - Fabric/clothing materials
  - Other manufactured goods
- **Secrecy:** Uses Mental Erasure ability
  - No one at the settlement remembers him after he leaves
  - No records of transactions
  - Maintains complete anonymity
- **What Arjan & Lopney know:** Minimal
  - They know the boat exists
  - Gallan may tell them he's "checking the far side of the island" or "fishing"
  - They are not aware of his trips to civilization

#### **The Boat Details**
- Small vessel, handbuilt by Gallan from island timber
- Simple design - rowboat or small sailboat (TBD based on distance to mainland)
- Tied up on the shore near Gallan's home
- Functional but not sophisticated
- **Restriction:** Arjan and Lopney are forbidden from using it
  - Gallan's stated reason: "Too dangerous"
  - Real reason: Keeps them isolated from the outside world

### **ATMOSPHERE & TONE**

#### **Visual Character**
- Lush tropical paradise
- Dense green forest canopy
- White or golden sand beaches on calm shores
- Dramatic rocky cliffs on windward sides
- Morning mist rising from the forest
- Vibrant colors: tropical flowers, bright birds, clear blue water
- Stunning sunsets over the ocean
- Star-filled night sky (no light pollution)

#### **Emotional Tone**
- **Peaceful isolation** - haven from the outside world
- **Safe** - no external threats, no dangerous wildlife
- **Complete** - everything needed for survival is present
- **Limited** - confined world, no access to broader civilization
- **Beautiful** - natural wonder, hence "A Wondrous Island"
- **Bittersweet:**
  - For Gallan: Refuge and redemption, but also self-imposed exile
  - For Arjan: Perfect home, doesn't want to leave
  - For Lopney: Beautiful prison, desperately wants to explore beyond

#### **Sensory Details**
- **Sound:** Ocean waves, tropical bird calls, rustling leaves, rain on forest canopy
- **Smell:** Salt air, tropical flowers, damp earth after rain
- **Feel:** Warm humid air, cool river water, rough tree bark, soft sand
