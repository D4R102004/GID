## GALLAN'S PAST

### **THE SECRET:**
- Gallan Byronstrike was a Pristania Apostle (served Hirohiko)
- He was a high-ranking member, present during the war
- His Nouryoku: Mental Erasure (can erase memories of himself)
- He defected after the peace treaty, disappeared to the island
- He's been hiding for 15 years, raising Arjan and Lopney

### **WHO KNOWS (Full Truth):**
- **Pristania leadership** (the Kamigami know he defected)
- **Glayne Byronstrike** (his brother - current Pristania Apostle)
- **Former Pristania members** who knew him

### **WHO KNOWS (Partial Truth):**
- **Free leadership** - May know the NAME "Gallan Byronstrike" as a defector
  - Don't know where he is
  - Don't know about the kids

### **WHO DOESN'T KNOW:**
- **Arjan & Lopney** (think Gallan is just "Dad" with no past)
- Most of Free (low-ranking members)
- The world at large (Mental Erasure hides him)

### **WHO SUSPECTS:**
- **Lopney** (more perceptive, senses he's hiding something)
- **Arjan** (naive, trusts Gallan completely - doesn't suspect)

### **WHO COULD DISCOVER IT:**
- Glayne (if he breaks through Mental Erasure and finds the island)
- Free members (if Gallan reveals himself or is recognized)
- The Kamigami (if they meet)

### **WHEN REVEALED (Planned):**
- **Arc 1:** Glayne discovers the island - forces Gallan's past into the open
- **To Arjan/Lopney:** During or after Glayne's attack

### **DRAMATIC IRONY:**
- Arjan and Lopney trust their "peaceful" father completely
- Gallan is haunted by his past but can't tell them
- He raised them to be good BECAUSE he was bad
- His skills (combat, training) come from his dark past

## THE RESURRECTION TRUTH

### **THE SECRET:**

- Has one use left but fears it will kill him

### **WHO KNOWS (The Truth):**
- **No one living**
  - Hide is dead (took the truth with him)
  - The Ryakujin may know (if they still exist)

### **WHO KNOWS (The Lie):**
- **Kami** (deceased - died believing the lie)
- **The Kamigami** - Kami told them the lie
  - They believe resurrection costs the user's life after two uses
- **Jashin** - May or may not know (unclear if Hide told him)

### **WHO DOESN'T KNOW (Either Version):**
- All of Free
- All of Pristania (except the Kamigami)
- Arjan & Lopney
- Rajin/Megami (she was dead when Hide told the lie)

### **WHEN REVEALED (Planned):**
- [TBD - Major twist potential]
- [TBD - If Arjan/Lopney use resurrections, do they discover the truth?]

### **DRAMATIC IRONY:**
- Kami had the power to resurrect one more person but was too afraid
