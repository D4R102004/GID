## **PHYSICAL DESCRIPTION**

**Build:**
- Tall, delicate, gracious woman
- Very beautiful - striking presence
- Does NOT train in combat (relies purely on Nouryoku)
- Elegant, refined physique
- Somewhat fragile physically

**Face & Hair:**
- **White long hair** - curled using traditional Japanese hair adjuster (kanzashi/kōgai style)
- **Light orange eyes** - distinctive, warm color
- Resembles her mother Taeko strongly
- Beautiful, ethereal appearance
- Can make adorable/confused expressions despite maturity

**Distinctive Style:**
- **Traditional Japanese aesthetic** - fully embraces Jitsumoto's culture
- **White kimono** with **blue obi**
- **Long socks** (tabi) to hide delicate legs
- **Hair ornaments** - multiple decorative pieces in traditional Japanese style
- **Signature item:** Hair adjuster/kanzashi used to curl her hair
- **No armor** - fights in her kimono

**Body Language:**
- **Default:** Authoritative, commanding presence
- **Eerie quality:** Combination of dress, white hair, expression creates unsettling beauty
- **Dual nature:** Can behave like schoolgirl despite being in her 30s
- **With strangers:** Maintains strong, imposing impression
- **With loved ones:** Warm, docile, loving
- **When angry:** Explosive violence, rising voice, sudden outbursts
- **Hard to approach** but capable of great warmth

**Overall Impression:**
- Gracious, powerful, beautiful
- Eerie ethereal quality
- Contradictions: delicate but ruthless, authoritative but playful
- Spoiled princess energy (お嬢様 - ojou-sama)

## **POWERS & COMBAT**

### **Nouryoku: Logia (Tornado Manipulation)**

**Base Abilities:**

1. **Tornado Generation:**
   - Casts tornados from her hands
   - Can send them away and activate remotely
   - **Activation:** Tornados burst with air pressure, pulverizing targets
   - **Multiple tornados** can be deployed simultaneously

2. **Flight:**
   - Can fly using tornado propulsion
   - Maneuverability in air combat

3. **Tornado Charge:**
   - Gets inside a tornado herself
   - Charges at enemies at devastating speed
   - **Effect:** Pierces through targets or rips apart flesh
   - **Usually insta-kills** - brutally effective

4. **Tornado Prison:**
   - Creates circle of tornados around enemies
   - Traps them, prevents escape
   - Tornados close in and encase target
   - **Total burst** - pulverizes everything inside

5. **Underwater Breathing:**
   - Engulfs herself in tornado
   - Can dive underwater while protected
   - Breathes inside tornado bubble

**Signature Technique: Aeronaval Gigablast**
- **Execution:** Pushes both hands forward
- Releases **two massive tornados** simultaneously
- Tornados engulf and pulverize everything in path
- **Devastating finisher move**

---

### **Hidden Ability (Discovered Arc 5):**

**True Nature: She IS the Tornado**

**What This Means:**
- Yonoa herself can **become a tornado**
- Not just controlling them - she transforms into one
- **Regeneration:** Can absorb nearby air to regenerate
- **Lost limbs can be restored** by drawing in air
- **Near-immortality** in combat if air is available

**Discovery:**
- Doesn't know about this until **Arc 5**
- Revelation during critical battle
- Game-changer for her survival/power level

---

### **Power Assessment:**

**Strengths:**
- **Absolutely overpowered ability**
- No real limitations on tornado creation
- Versatile (offense, defense, mobility, utility)
- Regeneration makes her nearly unkillable
- Flight gives tactical advantage
- Area control through tornado prison

**Limitations:**
- **Close-range vulnerability** - if enemy gets in close, she has little defense
- **Physically fragile** - not trained in hand-to-hand combat
- **No martial arts training** - relies entirely on Nouryoku
- **No weapon skills** - pure ability user

---

### **Combat Philosophy:**

**Approach:**
- **Fights to destroy and annihilate**
- Brutal, overwhelming power
- No hesitation, no mercy
- **Fights to kill** - leaves no loose ends
- Doesn't play with enemies (unlike some fighters)

**Influenced by Anger:**

- **Reckless** - doesn't calculate risks well

- **Easily provoked** - can be baited into mistakes

**Can Fight Calm:**
- **Yes** - capable of controlled combat

**Tactics:**

**Strengths:**

**Weaknesses:**
