# **TASED (LEANDRYS OCHOA) - COMPLETE CHARACTER PROFILE**

---

## **BASIC INFO**

**Full Name:** Leandrys Ochoa
**Nickname:** Tased (多 + seeds - "Many Seeds")
**Pronunciation:** TAH-sed (not "tay-seed")
**Age:** 17 years old (Arc 2)
**Occupation:** Free Security Trainee
**Status:** War orphan raised by Tairin, plant manipulator, tragic figure

---

## **PHYSICAL DESCRIPTION**

**Build:**
- Skinny, athletic
- Average height for 17
- Fast-moving, agile physique
- Built for speed and precision, not power

**Face & Hair:**
- **Short green hair**
- **Yellow eyes** - bright, observant
- Young face but carries himself with experience
- Not inexperienced-looking despite his youth

**Distinctive Features:**
- **Always has a plant in his mouth** - chewing/holding it like a toothpick
- Habit, same type of plant every time
- Keeps it even during combat
- Has plants in his office, loves gardening

**Overall Impression:**
- **Stoic and calm** - rarely shows emotion
- Carries quiet confidence
- Looks like he knows more than he does
- "Cool dude" energy - nonchalant, unbothered
- Until he loses it - then he's terrifying

**Body Language:**
- Stoic, still, doesn't gesticulate much
- Moves fluidly (like a mantis when fighting)
- Doesn't scream unless furious
- Quiet confidence in his stance
- Relaxed posture (flows with life)

## **PERSONALITY CORE**

### **Defining Traits:**
1. **Serene and all-knowing** - acts like he knows more than you, flows with life
2. **Stoic observer** - quiet, doesn't speak much, spits facts when he does
3. **Hidden rage** - calm exterior hides burning desire for revenge
4. **Sassy joker** - makes funny remarks from behind ("Mrs. Mommy," "The Psychic Lord")
5. **Tragic victim** - war orphan consumed by need to avenge his parents

### **Core Values:**
- **Avenging his parents** - killed by Pristania Apostle Gallan Byronstrike
- **Free's cause** - believes in the ideology, loyal to the organization
- **Duty and focus** - takes his training and role seriously
- **Living happily** - wants peace despite his thirst for revenge

### **Fatal Flaws:**
- **Obsessed with revenge** - will throw his life away for it
- **Inexperienced** - only 17, not ready for the enemies he faces
- **Lets rage override logic** - calm facade breaks when facing his parents' killers
- **Won't let go of the past** - can't move forward, stuck in grief

### **Dual Nature:**

**The Calm Stoic (Default):**
- Nonchalant, serene, flows with life
- Sassy remarks, doesn't take things too seriously
- Quiet confidence, all-knowing stance
- Respectful but playful with authority

**The Enraged Avenger (When Triggered):**
- **Explosive fury** - loses all composure
- Aggressive, violent, desperate
- Single-minded focus on revenge
- Will sacrifice himself for vengeance

### **What Drives Him:**
- **Primary:** Getting revenge for his parents' deaths
- **Secondary:** Proving himself in Security, living up to their legacy
- **Hidden:** Just wanting to be happy, to move on (but can't)
- **Denied:** Letting go of the past and finding peace

### **What Makes Him Happy:**
- Gardening (plants, flowers, nature)
- Training and improving
- Hanging out with Light (his friend)
- Being acknowledged as capable
- Moments of peace in Free HQ

### **What Enrages Him:**
- **Pristania** (his parents' killers)
- **The name "Byronstrike"** (Gallan killed his parents)
- Injustice and dishonor
- Being underestimated (though he hides this well)

## **POWERS & COMBAT**

### **Nouryoku: Plant Manipulation**

**Nickname Origin:** Tased (多 + seeds) - "Many Seeds"

**Core Ability:**
- **Generates plants from nothing** (doesn't need existing plants)
- Can manipulate and control plants at a distance
- Grows them **rather fast** (seconds to minutes depending on size)
- Creates grass, leaves, flowers, branches, thorns, bushes, vines

**Scale:**
- Can fill a **tiny room** with plants normally
- In an **outburst of rage**, can fill a **small baseball field**
- That's his maximum output

**Applications:**
1. **Restraint** - vines and roots to bind enemies
2. **Defense** - bushes and thick vegetation as barriers
3. **Offense** - thorns on the floor, piercing branches
4. **Battlefield control** - blocks passages, creates cover
5. **Trap setting** - hidden thorns, surprise vines

---

### **Combat Style - The Mantis Fighter:**

**Signature Technique - Mantis Claws:**
- **Grows branches on his arms** that harden into mantis-like claws
- Made of **hardened wood**
- Very strong and pointy
- Can **cut through flesh** and resist mid-to-light blows
- Can **retract them** when not needed
- Uses them for **surgical, piercing strikes**

**Fighting Approach:**
- **Moves like a mantis** - quick, surgical strikes
- **Creates a "garden battlefield"** around himself
- Overwhelms opponents with plants while attacking with claws
- **Doesn't give them leeway** - constant pressure from all sides
- **Stealth and surgical attacks** - not a brawler, a precision fighter

**Strengths:**
- High combat capability (despite being 17)
- Very fast (hard to beat on speed alone)
- Creative use of plants (versatile)
- Deadly with his mantis claws
- Strategic mind (uses environment)

**Weaknesses:**
- **Fragile plants** - can be overpowered by brute force
- **Fire** - burns his plants, neutralizes his power
- **Ice** - freezes and kills his plants
- **Poison** - anything that kills plants counters him
- **Inexperience** - only 17, underdeveloped skills
- **Opponents who overpower him** - struggles against stronger enemies

---

### **Combat Capability:**

**Overall Assessment:**
- High capability **for his age**
- Complex fighting style (takes skill to master)
- A bit weak due to inexperience
- Deadly against mid-tier opponents
- Struggles against top-tier fighters (like Pristania Emperors)

**What He's Learning:**
- Ruthlessness from Cristopher (loses some calm in combat)
- Tactical thinking from training sessions
- How to push through fear and pain
- How to work with teammates (Arjan training)

## **THEMATIC ROLE**

### **What Tased Represents:**

1. **The Tragedy of War:**
   - War orphan consumed by cycle of violence
   - Parents killed, he seeks revenge, dies trying
   - Cycle continues - violence begets violence

2. **Revenge Gets You Nothing:**
   - Dies **without getting revenge**
   - His obsession destroys him
   - Lesson: letting go is survival, clinging to revenge is death

3. **The Cost of Obsession:**
   - Calm and capable until revenge consumes him
   - Could have had a future (friends, training, happiness)
   - Threw it all away for vengeance he never got

4. **Hope Destroyed:**
   - Seen as "ray of hope" for Free's future
   - Promise and potential cut short
   - War doesn't care about potential - it kills indiscriminately

5. **Incomplete Resolution:**
   - Final words cut off ("Mom, Dad, I...")
   - Never finishes his thought
   - Dies reaching for something he'll never have
   - Life is messy, death doesn't wait for closure
